About Us

Your Neighborhood Mechanic

Pro-Choice Auto Care is a trusted, owner-operated repair shop in Missouri City, TX. Under the leadership of owner Lee Ramos — a skilled, knowledgeable mechanic with over a decade of hands-on experience — we provide honest diagnostics, transparent pricing, and reliable repairs for all makes and models.

Unlike dealerships and chain shops that upsell unnecessary services, Lee tells you exactly what your vehicle needs and what it doesn't. He's fixed problems that dealerships couldn't diagnose — and done it for a fraction of the price.
